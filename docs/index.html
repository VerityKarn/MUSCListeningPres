Musical Stereotype

Country is boring guitar music about tradition and rural life

Jazz is an unorganized mess

Rap is basically rythmic talking about gangster life

Classical is boring music made for snobs

Metal is angry guitar and demons

Focusing on Metal Stereotype

Metal expression is both technical and metaphorical

Technical expression through intense virtuosity

Metaphorical expression through dark and vague imagery

Problem of metaphor versus interpretation

"Metal speaks of demons, therefore it is demonic"

Tipper Gore hearings on Metal

Metal fans versus outsiders

Outsiders perspective:

  • Demonic themes
  • Chaotic, harsh, and dissonant tones
  • Violent and evil

Metal fans perspective:

  • Demons = evil forces within ourselves and others
  • Life is chaotic, harsh, and dissonant
  • The world is violent and evil, and we wish to understand it

Important musical elements of Metal

Emphasis on tonal complexity

Emphasis on temporal modularity

Emphasis on difficult and vague metaphor

What I selected for

Relative tonal simplicity

Simple temporal sense

Explainable metaphor

Song 1

Love The Subhuman Self

Theme of Millia Rage

Millia's Story

  • Grew up in Assassin's Guild
  • Experimented on as a child
  • Questionable relationship with Zato=1
  • Became Director of PWAB

Main Ideas of Theme

  • Anger and Doubts about herself
  • Anger and Doubts about Zato
  • Must move forward regardless

Main Musical Elements of Theme

  • Main Sound: Harsh, Fast, Angry Guitar and Electronics
  • Underlying Sound: Softer, Accentuating Strings and Piano
  • Main Thematic Throughline: Unforgiving and angry
  • Subtle Thematic Throughline: Pleading and sad

Song 2

Like A Weed, Naturally, as a Matter of Course

Theme of Testament

Testament's Story

  • Kidnapped and experimented on for genetic weapon projects
  • Possessed by Justice in order to destroy the world
  • Afterwards, tried to help Justice's innocent daughter, Dizzy
  • Spent the last 7 years in solitude to discover themself

Main Ideas of Theme

  • All life is connected
  • Life is neither good or evil
  • Life is worth living for its own sake
  • Testament doesn't want to change the world, but observe it

Main Musical Elements of Theme

  • Main Sound: Energetic, Rising Vocals, Guitar, and Strings
  • Underlying Sound: Guitar and Bass in Gallop Step
  • Main Thematic Throughline: Triumphant, Joyful Rising
  • Subtle Thematic Throughline: Fast but Grounded

Song 3

Drift

Theme of Happy Chaos

Chaos' Story

  • Original Discoverer of Magic and Backyard(higher dimension controlling the universe)
  • Secluded himself in the Backyard
  • "Stare into the abyss, and it stares back"
  • Began to believe that Chaos was the catalyst for greater life
  • Influenced events from the Backyard to create Chaos

Main Ideas of Theme

  • There is no "Truth"
  • Everyone's perspective makes a new truth
  • Everything is a story, and HC doesn't want it to ever end
  • No defined motivations, except to keep the story going forever
  • "Please don't stop the flow"

Main Musical Elements of Theme

    Best understood as different forms of the smile

  • Suave smile with a swagger
  • Hazy, but slightly deranged smile
  • Manic, cackling, terrifying smile
  • Hopeful, estatic smile

Seriously, this is the best way to explain it
